Project Details

Commercial Backup System

For this enterprise client, downtime was not an option. We designed and installed a robust commercial backup power system capable of sustaining critical operations during extended grid outages. The solution integrates high-capacity battery storage with smart switchgear for seamless transition.

Project Aims

The primary requirement was to ensure zero interruption to server rooms and communications infrastructure. The system also needed to be scalable to accommodate future expansion of the facility.

Challenges

Integrating with the building's legacy electrical distribution board required careful planning and custom fabrication of busbars.

  • Seamless Transfer Switch
  • Scalable Battery Bank
  • Remote Monitoring

Flora Solar & Tech empowers businesses to maintain productivity regardless of grid instability. Our commercial solutions are built for reliability and ROI.
