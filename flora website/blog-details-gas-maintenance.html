Why Regular Maintenance is Key for Gas Appliances

Gas stoves, heaters, and water geysers are robust appliances that can last for years. However, like any machine, they require regular attention to operate safely and efficiently. Neglecting maintenance can lead to gas leaks, inefficiency, and potential hazards.

Clogged jets are a common issue. Food spills or dust can block the tiny holes where gas comes out, leading to a weak or yellow flame. Regular cleaning with warm soapy water and a soft brush can prevent this.
For gas geysers, it's important to check the flue pipe for obstructions like bird nests, which can cause dangerous carbon monoxide buildup.

At Flora Gases, we offer inspection and maintenance services to give you peace of mind. Taking care of your appliances ensures they take care of you.
