Instant LPG Refills & Exchange

Cooking shouldn't stop because of a power cut or an empty tank. At Flora Gas, we provide a 'While-You-Wait' rapid refill service using high-precision digital scales. Whether you need a top-up for your 3kg cooker or a full refill for a 48kg industrial cylinder, we guarantee accurate weight and safety-checked cylinders every time.

The Refill Process

Our 3-step quality guarantee.

Safety Inspection

We check your cylinder for leaks and valve integrity before filling.

Precision Fill

We use calibrated digital scales. You only pay for the gas we put in.

Quality Seal

Every cylinder is shrink-sealed to guarantee quantity and safety.

Cylinders We Accept

We refill all major branded cylinders provided they are within their safety test date.

  • Domestic: 3kg, 5kg, 6kg, 9kg (Camping & Home).

  • Standard: 14kg, 19kg (Home Heating & Stove).

  • Industrial: 48kg (Hotels & Restaurants).

Why Choose Flora Gas?

  • No Under-Filling: Digital accuracy means you get exactly what you pay for.

  • Partial Refills: Short on cash? We can fill $5 or $10 worth of gas into any size cylinder.

  • Safety First: Free O-ring replacement with every refill if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do cylinder swaps?

Yes! If you don't want to wait for a refill, swap your standard empty cylinder for a pre-filled Flora Gas cylinder instantly.

Can you refill South African/Foreign cylinders?

Yes, we have adapters for most regional valves (Totai, Cadac, etc.).

How do I know it's full?

We weigh the cylinder in front of you—Empty Weight + Gas Weight = Total.
